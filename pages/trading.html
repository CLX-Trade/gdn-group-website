How We Work

Our Trade Process

Every GDN transaction follows a verified, documented process — protecting both buyers and sellers from enquiry to settlement.

01
Enquiry & LOI
Buyer submits enquiry with volumes, destination, and preferred delivery terms. GDN issues Soft Corporate Offer (SCO) within 48 hours.
02
ICPO & Verification
Buyer returns Irrevocable Corporate Purchase Order (ICPO). Both parties complete KYC and entity verification.
03
Contract Execution
Sale & Purchase Agreement (SPA) executed. Payment instrument (DLC/SBLC/MT103) established through buyer's bank.
04
SGS Inspection
Independent SGS or Intertek inspection at load port confirms product quality against SPA specifications.
05
Shipment & Documentation
Full shipping documents (B/L, COO, COA, SGS report) issued. Vessel nomination and port coordination managed.
06
Settlement
Payment released against shipping documents. GDN coordinate any post-delivery queries and repeat order scheduling.
